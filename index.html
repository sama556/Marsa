About Marsa

Welcome to Marsa

Marsa is a web-based interactive platform dedicated to showcasing Saudi Arabia’s unique Red Sea islands, from Farasan and Shura to emerging regenerative tourism destinations.

We connect travelers with curated island experiences, certified local operators, and eco-conscious activities. Discover snorkeling trips, boat tours, cultural walks, and more – all in a single, easy-to-use interface tailored for the Saudi islands.
