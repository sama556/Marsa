Marsa Privacy & Data Protection

This page explains how Marsa – Web-Based Interactive Platform for Exploring Saudi Islands collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you browse the platform or create an account.

Information we collect

We collect only the information that is necessary to provide you with a smooth booking experience and to operate our services safely.

  • Account details such as name, username, email, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Contact details like phone number if you choose to provide it.
  • Booking information including islands, activities, dates, number of people, and payments status.
  • Technical data such as IP address, device type, and basic analytics used to improve performance.
How we use your data
  • To create and manage your Marsa account and bookings.
  • To communicate about confirmations, updates, or changes related to your trips and activities.
  • To keep the platform secure, prevent fraud, and monitor suspicious activity.
  • To improve the user experience, for example by understanding which islands and activities are most popular.
Cookies & similar technologies

Marsa may use cookies or similar technologies to remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and measure how the platform is used. You can control cookies from your browser settings, but some features may not work correctly if you disable them completely.

Third‑party services

In some cases we work with trusted third‑party providers (for example, secure payment gateways, analytics tools, or cloud hosting). Those providers only receive the minimum data required to perform their services and are not allowed to use your data for their own marketing.

Your rights
  • Access: you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Update: you can correct or update your profile information from your account settings.
  • Delete: you can ask to delete your account, subject to legal or regulatory obligations.
  • Control communication: you can manage notification preferences for marketing or updates.
Security

We use industry‑standard security practices to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and secure password hashing. No method is 100% secure, but we continually review and improve our security controls.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy & Policy page from time to time to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. When we make a significant change, we will notify you through the platform or by email where appropriate.

Contact us

If you have any questions about how we handle your data, you can contact the Marsa support team:

  • Email: privacy@marsa.sa
  • Subject line: “Marsa Privacy Request”
